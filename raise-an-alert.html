Disaster Response

Levels of Disaster

Level 1: Minor Incident

  • Localized impact
  • Minimal damage to property
  • No injuries or fatalities
  • Requires minimal response and resources

Level 2: Moderate Incident

  • Moderate impact over a wider area
  • Some property damage
  • Possible minor injuries
  • Involves local emergency services

Level 3: Serious Incident

  • Significant impact with potential for major disruption
  • Substantial property damage
  • Injuries requiring medical attention
  • May require regional response and coordination

Level 4: Major Disaster

  • Widespread impact affecting multiple communities
  • Extensive property damage
  • Multiple injuries or fatalities
  • Requires national or international assistance

Level 5: Catastrophic Disaster

  • Severe, long-term impact on a large area
  • Massive destruction of property
  • High number of casualties and fatalities
  • Extensive humanitarian assistance and recovery efforts needed
